Our Story

The present CEO & MD Mr. Rabiul Islam has been globally consulting as a cybersecurity specialist since 2008. He never thought to start his career journey in the cybersecurity field, but having an immense amount of passion in cybersecurity, he quickly became a skilled cybersecurity professional.

Finally, in 2014, he and Ms. Rehena Parveen established TechForing as a private limited company in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Since then, we have set up our regional offices in Istanbul- Turkey, Michigan- USA and London- UK. In 2020, more like-minded partners, i.e. Mr. Phillip R Moran and Mr. Mahbub Choudhury, joined the board of directors.

At present, TechForing employs 150+ experienced in-house cybersecurity specialists distributed in four continents. With the vast resource pool of security researchers experienced in a wide range of domains, we stand out in this high cyberattack-prone IT industry.
